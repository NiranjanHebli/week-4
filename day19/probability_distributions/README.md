## Part B - Stretch Problem 

This section compares normal and uniform distributions, demonstrating how values are concentrated around the mean in normal distributions and spread evenly in uniform distributions.

## Key Learnings

- Different distributions represent different real world uncertainty patterns.

- Histogram shape helps identify underlying probability behavior.

- Mean and standard deviation alone do not fully explain distribution structure.

- Normal distribution is useful for natural phenomena and statistical inference.

- Uniform distribution is useful when every outcome has equal chance.
